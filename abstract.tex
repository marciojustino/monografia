The pourpose of this research is to explain and determine a better way to locating fragments of unallocated files during the file carving on digital
forensics addressing the concept methodology for identification of fragments of files and the benefits it provides for analysis in a investigation of
a digital image evidence when possible to locate parts, thus allowing their identification.

\textbf{Key-words:} NTFS, fragmentation, carving, file, entropy.
